Do not throw it away, give it away

Every year tons of reusable products are thrown away and they can be given a second chance

Reasons

Recycling is one of the keys to sustainability. And there is no recycling more efficient than the second-hand market. The growing awareness of climate change and the environment is triggering the use of buying and selling platforms. According to the "Second Hand Effect" report, the method used to calculate the potential benefit for the climate derived from the second-hand market is based on the assumption that each used item sold replaces the production of a new equivalent item, as well as the management of the waste of said product. That is, if a person buys a used bicycle instead of a new one, there will be no need to produce a new one and, in addition, the used one will not have to be processed to turn it into waste. Reyours understanding this problem arises as a channel that connects people who want to donate an article that, even being second hand, is still useful and therefore could be used by another person who needs it. Reyours allows the virtual communication of people who, although unknown, are aware of the problems of climate change and other factors of social nature so they accept with pleasure the acquisition and donation of second-hand items

Features

Create an Announcement

Do you have an article to give? With Reyours you can create easily and without the need to register announcements and share with the community.

Update Images

a picture is worth a thousand words so you can add an image of the article you want to donate to your announcement

Edit/Delete Announcements

You can easily edit or delete announcements so you can tell others about news

Search and Filter Announcements

If you are looking for an article you can easily access all the announcements in our database, you can even filter the announcements by title and finalization date.

send a request to the donor user

In case you are interested in an announcement you can send an email directly from the website to the donor user, in this way he or she can contact you
